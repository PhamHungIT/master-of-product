Welcome

The goal of this website is to help customers find the perfect laptop.
This is my project for my Data Science course at Hanoi University of Science and Technology.
Laptops come in a wide variety of sizes, features, and prices, which makes choosing the best laptop a challenge. That's why you need to figure out what your needs are.

I hope that this website will help you find the best one.

Future extensions to this website could include:

  • Collecting dataset from more websites
  • Tooltips to explain terms and components used for laptops
  • Creating viusal representation of laptop market based on dataset
  • Cleaning up the code
